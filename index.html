CarPool For Students

In this project, we created an application for students who are new to campus and the place and don't have the proper transportation to get basic groceries or any amenities. I too was an international student who struggled to go out and get things at first since places were far away from my campus. And all I thought was " Man! I wish I knew someone who has a car who could give me a ride!". To solve this difficulty, we decided to create an application in which students who have a car can post if they are going to the store and say that they could take two or more people with them. And then the student who doesn't have transport can connect with the student and plan their trip to the store. The application is a social media type, where multiple students can post and students can connect with them!
